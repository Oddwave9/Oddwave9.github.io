Frequently Asked Questions(FAQ)

Here are quick answers to some of your questions.

CAN YOU WRITE ON A CAKE?

Yes, with advance notice we can write a message on your cake.

HOW MANY PEOPLE DOES A CAKE FEED?

It depends on how hungry your group is! In the bakery, we slice our cakes into ten pieces. You can definitely get up to 12 generous slices from one of our signature cakes.

CAN I INCLUDE A PERSONALIZED GIFT MESSAGE IN MY ORDER?

Yes, depending on your preference. It could be hand-written notes, custom-printed cards or a simple typed message on the packing slip to be included in each gift.

DO YOU OFFER GIFT-WRAPPING?

Yes, for a small incremental cost we will wrap your gift with our signature satin ribbon and include a hand-written note.

CAN I SELECT MY DELIVERY DATE?

Yes, you can. Once an order is placed we will agree on a set delivery date and would get it delivered without any hassles.
